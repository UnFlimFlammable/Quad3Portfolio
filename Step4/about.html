Dan Keller

Dan Keller is a columnist for the Boulder Times. His 2011 series "All that and More" earned him Boulder’s prestigious “Up-and-Comer” award for innovation. When he isn’t glued to a computer screen, he spends time working in the garden, learning French, and trying very hard not be the worst pool player in the Rockies. Dan Keller is interested in backend java with Spring, though he is competent on the front end as well. Dan has worked extensively in school with Java Spring Framework, and AngularJS. He is also familiar with Node.js, and Express. Dan received many accolades on his work with the Google Maps API
